OUR STORY

You may be wondering who we are here at Catzen Coffee. We are Vanessa and Frances, two friends who started with an idea that has blossomed into this quirky little coffee shop. Vanessa is originally from the Hawkeye State, but hopes you don't hold that against her. She has lived in Saint Paul for the past 30 years, and is proud to call it her home. When she's not wrangling cats at Catzen, she is home with her spouse, teenager, two dogs, and two cats. Her hobbies include ice dipping, history, and Russian literature. Frances is a Midwesterner who enjoys cooking, sharing meals, and spending time outdoors with her partner and their two dogs. Whether it's a homemade dish or a day in nature, Frances enjoys the simple things in life. We hope to bring a smile to all who stop by Catzen Coffee. Our goal is to lift up our neighborhood and our local businesses and to create a warm and welcoming place to learn and gather together. We are happy you are here!
