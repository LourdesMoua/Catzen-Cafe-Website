CATZEN COFFEE

OUR CAFE & THE LIVING ROOM

We are a specialty coffee shop where you can grab an expertly brewed espresso, cup of coffee, or tea. We also offer quick bites to eat such as local pastries and hand pies.

We have additional seating in our living room, a special cozy refuge where our 8 Catzen Cats roam. The cats are free to move between the living room and their basement living quarters, so you may see them or not, depending on their mood. You know how cats are.

The living room is an extension of our coffee shop which offers additional seating. Enjoy cozy nooks, a heated floor, a welcoming fireplace, and our resident Catzen Cats, who may decide to join you. Grab your coffee and snacks, a book from the bookshelf, or a game from the game shelf, and settle in.

You must be 15 years or older to be in the living room, except for any kid times that may be scheduled (see below for exceptions).

Reservations for the living room are required. Reservations are 45 minutes long. There is no fee for a living room reservation, but we do ask that you make a purchase in the coffee shop for each person entering.
